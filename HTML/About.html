What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn. It includes technologies like machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.

History of AI

  • 1950s: Alan Turing proposes the Turing Test.
  • 1960s: First AI programs developed.
  • 2000s: Rise of machine learning and big data.
  • Today: AI powers self-driving cars, virtual assistants, and more.
